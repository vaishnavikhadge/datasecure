Privacy & Cookie Policy

Effective Date: June 9, 2025

1. Introduction

At DataSecure AI, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with our services, including website visits and AI-driven features.

2. Information We Collect

We collect both personal and non-personal data, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email, phone number
  • Billing and payment details
  • IP address, browser type, device identifiers
  • Usage behavior across our platform

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize services
  • Facilitate payments and customer support
  • Send important updates, newsletters (with consent)
  • Improve performance and AI recommendations

4. Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. Cookies help us:

  • Remember user preferences and sessions
  • Analyze site traffic using analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Provide secure logins and prevent fraud

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect functionality.

5. Data Sharing & Third-Party Tools

We do not sell your data. However, we share data with:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Razorpay)
  • Email service providers
  • Analytics and error monitoring services

These third parties are under confidentiality agreements and only process data on our behalf.

6. Data Security

We use SSL encryption, secure data storage, and role-based access controls. All payment information is handled securely via PCI-DSS compliant services.

7. Your Rights

Under laws like the GDPR and Indian IT Act, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request a copy of stored data

To request any of the above, contact us at support@datasecure.ai

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable data protection regulations including the EU GDPR, India’s IT Act, and relevant consumer protection laws.

10. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated occasionally. You will be notified of any significant changes via email or notice on the website.
